It is crucial for individuals, especially students and volunteers, to recognize the power of giving back to society. Through acts of love, care, and selflessness, we can transform countless lives and create a better world for all. Drawing inspiration from the teachings of various faiths and the wisdom of renowned figures like Mahatma Gandhi and Margaret Cho, we can reignite the spirit of giving and sharing. By incorporating these values into our daily lives, we possess the ability to make a lasting difference, one small gesture at a time. Let us embrace the opportunity to serve and uplift others, for in doing so, we not only enhance our own humanity but also contribute to the flourishing of society as a whole.

Volunteer benefits

As a volunteer you will get benefit from:

  • Training - get discounted rates on our unique training courses.
  • Events - receive discounted tickets and invitations to member-only events.
  • Community - share knowledge and best practice with peers.
  • Networking - talk to the right people when you need to.
  • Expertise - access the Bond groups and the best tools and resources.
