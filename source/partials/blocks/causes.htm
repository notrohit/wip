Primary Focus

CSJSF is a Public Charitable Trust focused on providing value-based education for disadvantaged children. It is committed to mentoring these children and aiding their growth. In addition to their primary education, we offer coaching, soft skills training, and leadership development. We also provide personal development, wellness programs, and comprehensive grooming. Our goal is to guide them until they become a productive human resource for the nation.
